Biography

Defined by their mystique, confessional lyrics, and harmonized vocals, Deeps is the emerging musical act from Sweden. “From day one, we have agreed that this project should not be about who we are as people or as artists, it should solely be about the music. We are all artists in our own way with unique stories to tell. Thus, we do not want to paint the whole picture for our listeners. We just want to provide them with the paintbrush and canvas, so they can create their own paintings.” With their debut album “Oceanside,” Deeps has introduced an inspirational voice and is bringing a fresh new sound into the the ever changing indie scene. Deeps is currently finalizing their second project, which is scheduled to be released in the fall.
